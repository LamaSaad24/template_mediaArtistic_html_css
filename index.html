what happens after you contact us?

step 1

our team responds

We reach out to schedule a no-cost no-commitment consultation meeting to get to know your needs. In this meeting, we’ll also share our expertise, values, history, and how it relates to your success.

step 2

meeting

You tell us about your project goals and challenges. Our business leaders will listen and ask questions to understand your business needs. Then we share our ideas on how to accomplish your goals.

step 3

discovery

We work with you, as well as independently, to analyze your technology. This helps us limit the overall assumptions we make when defining solutions for you.

step 4

solution proposal check-in

We circle back with you to confirm our understanding of the solution you require, report our findings, and recommend our approach in a project proposal.

step 5

we start building

Our developers and business leaders stay in contact with you throughout. The most successful projects have high collaboration, so we always maintain consistent communication.

step 6

deliver

After quality assurance and user acceptance testing, we launch your solution. We remain available to help with post-launch support.

what we promise

  • Media Artistic's biggest concern when choosing a technology is how it fits our customer's needs.
  • Media Artistic does not resell any platforms or technologies of the third party vendors. We promise that we never have any commercial incentive while choosing a technology or approach, we base our decision only on the fact how our customers would benefit from it.
  • Our customers' time is their money, so completing every project on time is one of Media Artistic's biggest priorities.
  • Our customers value fair and transparent pricing, so that's what Media Artistic offers.
